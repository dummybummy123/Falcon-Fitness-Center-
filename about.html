Common Excerises

The following are common excerises that we encourage our clients to do as a part of their daily excerise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body excerise to increase your strength and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop in a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forawrd again quicklu, and then jump up as long as you can.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To preform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio excerise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to your chest and then switch as quicly as you can (as though you are climbing a mountain)
